“Your Trusted Partner for Precision Cutting Tools & Industrial Equipment”

PT. Buana Arta Toolindo (BAT), established in 2004, is a trading company that provides a wide range of cutting tools and industrial equipment. BAT is a dynamic company that values its customers as business partners — growing and developing together through mutual collaboration. We have partnered with many well-known companies across Indonesia, serving both mass production and custom manufacturing industries.
Our customers operate in various sectors, including automotive parts, electronic components, moulds and dies, workshops, and more. We supply a comprehensive selection of cutting tools for milling and turning processes, such as inserts, holders, taps, drills, endmills, and ballnoses. In addition, we also provide other industrial equipment such as measuring tools, fire extinguishers (APAR), and clamping accessories to support production efficiency.
